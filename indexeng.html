From surplus to resource

ReBygg gives you full control of your materials - and new opportunities in what would otherwise go to waste


Who are we?

ReBygg is a company that offers a digital platform developed to make the construction industry more sustainable through smarter use of materials. The company was founded by a group of former civil engineering students from NTNU Trondheim, who during their studies gained insight into how many usable materials are wasted on construction sites.

Through projects, internships, and collaboration with the industry, we discovered a clear need for better overview and coordination of surplus materials. The idea emerged from a desire to make it easier to register, share, and reuse materials in real time - instead of having them thrown away or forgotten.

Today, we have developed the ReBygg app, which gives contractors full control over material flow on the construction site. By combining technology with practical project needs, the ReBygg app contributes to reduced costs, less waste, and a more circular construction industry.

How we work

WHAT

The ReBygg app provides full visibility of surplus materials on the construction site. Users can register, find, and share materials, reducing both waste and costs.

WHY

The construction industry generates large amounts of waste, even though many of the materials are fully usable. Through dialogue with the business sector, we see a clear need for better overview. The ReBygg app is designed to contribute to increased sustainability and smarter resource utilization.

HOW

The solution has been developed based on insights from the construction industry through surveys. We have used Revit to model the storage system, and the app is designed to function as a digital twin that provides better overview and digital interaction.
